The Berecons Story

Berecons LLC emerged from a time of uncertainty and despair—the COVID-19 pandemic 2020. Driven by a desire to make a difference, our pioneers saw an opportunity to address the technical issues that often plague our market segments. And so, Beyond Research & Consulting, or “BeReCons”, was born. As we evolved from research, content, and management consulting services and to engage our stakeholders more holistically, we expanded our scope of solutions to include an array of business suites, digital solutions, property, and project management.

We comprise over 20 consultants from ten countries, all bringing a strong portfolio of diverse expertise and cultural perspectives. Additionally, a five-member Board of Directors oversees the strategic direction and governance of our firm, ensuring alignment with our mission and values, while our management team focuses on day-to-day operations and implementing the Board’s directives, driving the firm towards its goals with efficiency and effectiveness.

Our firm is committed to upholding its core values in every project we undertake, and our team is dedicated to excellence and providing strategic solutions that ensure our client’s complete satisfaction. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients and stakeholders and boast of a 98 per cent client retention rate.

We are proud of our journey and are excited to continue growing and evolving with you.

Who We Are

We are a seasoned team of professionals with backgrounds in economics, anthropology, sociology, political science, business management, law, health, and IT. Cumulatively, the Berecons LLC team has several decades of cumulative experience and multidisciplinary expertise from over ten countries and across sectors. With a deep understanding of the sector’s intricacies and difficulties, we have an advantage in customising project deliverables to suit each client’s needs.

What We Do

We specialise in research, data analysis, data management, content writing, editing, and a wide range of legal, project, and business solutions, including business analysis, development, strategy, marketing, project design and management. We also offer a scope of IT and web services, including branding, graphic design, website design, development, and ERP solutions. Our customised strategic approaches are uniquely tailored to every client’s needs.

Our Approach

We dedicate ourselves to measurable, sustainable professional pursuits that deeply impact our benefactors and stakeholders. Our commitment is reflected in the careful vetting of 15 projects by our team leaders each quarter. This meticulous selection ensures that dedicated resources and rigorous attention to detail are applied, upholding the highest quality standards. Through this deliberate approach, we assess, prioritise, and execute projects with precision, ultimately maximising positive outcomes for our benefactors and stakeholders alike.

Our Commitment to Results

We commit ourselves to delivering exceptional results that are meaningful, impactful, and measurable. Through our comprehensive approach to business programming, Berecons delivers practical and strategic solutions, ongoing support, and continuous evaluation to achieve successful outcomes.

Our Philosophy

Our work philosophy centres on delivering high-quality business consulting services, prioritising practical and strategic solutions, project success, and client satisfaction. We are convinced that the best publicity is recommendations from satisfied clients.
